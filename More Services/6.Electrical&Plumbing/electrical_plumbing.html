Electrical and Plumbing Installations: Ensuring Safety and Efficiency in Your Home

At Nuhomes Corp., we understand the importance of having safe and efficient electrical and plumbing systems in your home. Whether you're building a new home or renovating your existing one, our team of licensed electricians and plumbers can help ensure your systems are up to code and functioning properly.

Our electrical installation services include everything from wiring and lighting installation to panel upgrades and electrical repairs. We specialize in both residential and commercial electrical installations and offer a wide range of services to meet all your needs. Our team of licensed electricians has the experience and expertise to handle any project, big or small.

In addition to our installation services, we also offer maintenance and repair services for both electrical and plumbing systems. We believe that regular maintenance is the key to ensuring that your systems are functioning properly and efficiently, which can save you money in the long run. If you're experiencing any issues with your electrical or plumbing systems, our team of experts is available to provide prompt and reliable repairs.

Electrical Installations?

When it comes to electrical installations, safety should always be the top priority. Faulty wiring or outdated electrical systems can pose serious risks to your home and family. Our team of electricians has the expertise to design and install safe and efficient electrical systems that meet all relevant safety codes and standards.

    We offer a range of electrical services, including:
  • Electrical system design and installation for new homes
  • Electrical system upgrades for existing homes
  • Electrical panel upgrades
  • Circuit breaker replacements
  • Lighting installation
  • Generator installation and maintenance

No matter what your electrical needs may be, we have the expertise and experience to get the job done right.

Plumbing Installations

Just like electrical systems, plumbing systems are a critical component of any home. Leaks, clogs, and other plumbing issues can cause extensive damage to your home and disrupt your daily routine. Our team of licensed plumbers can help you design and install efficient plumbing systems that meet your specific needs.

    We offer a range of plumbing services, including:
  • Plumbing system design and installation for new homes
  • Plumbing system upgrades for existing homes
  • Water heater installation and repair
  • Pipe repair and replacement
  • Fixture installation (toilets, sinks, showers, etc.)
  • Drain cleaning and clog removal

Our team uses the latest technology and techniques to ensure your plumbing systems are functioning efficiently and effectively.

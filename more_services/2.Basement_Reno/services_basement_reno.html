Improved Home Value

When you work with NuHomes Corp, we will help you create a space that is not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing. Professional Guidance and Support

Basement Renovation Services by NuHomes Corp

At NuHomes Corp, we specialize in providing comprehensive Basement Renovation services that cater to your specific requirements. Our skilled team of experts will work with you to create a personalized design that matches your lifestyle, budget, and goals.

  • Basement Design: We collaborate with you to design a space that caters to your specific needs and preferences.
  • Basement Finishing: We use premium quality materials to finish your basement and create a space that is both functional and aesthetically pleasing. Our finishing services include drywall installation, flooring installation, ceiling installation, and painting.

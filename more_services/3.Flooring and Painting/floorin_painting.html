Premium Flooring Services

The foundation of any room's design is its flooring. The right choice can instantly elevate your space, bringing a sense of warmth, sophistication, or modernity, depending on your preferences. At NuHomes Corp., we offer a wide range of flooring options, from the timeless elegance of hardwood to the durability of tile and the comfort of carpet.

Our team of expert craftsmen are adept at installing new floors with utmost precision and care, ensuring you receive a final product that's both beautiful and built to last. But our services don't stop at installation. We also offer floor repairing and refinishing services to restore the original allure of your existing floors, extending their lifespan and saving you the cost of a full replacement.

Expert Painting Services

Color has the power to transform a space, evoke emotions, and create an ambiance. That's why our professional painting services are designed to bring your vision to life. Our team is experienced in painting various surfaces, including walls, ceilings, and trim, both inside and out.

Whether you desire a fresh, vibrant hue to energize a room or a subtle, soothing palette for a relaxing retreat, we have the skills and expertise to deliver an impeccable finish. Using high-quality paints and industry-leading techniques, we ensure a flawless and long-lasting result that will refresh your home and reflect your unique style.

In addition to new paint jobs, we offer repainting services to refresh tired, dull, or outdated colors. We meticulously prepare each surface to ensure optimal paint adherence and finish, resulting in a revitalized appearance that can dramatically boost your home's curb appeal and value.

Comprehensive Construction and Renovation Services at Nuhomes Corp.

Building or renovating a home is a significant investment, involving substantial time and effort to ensure that the end result meets your unique needs and preferences. At Nuhomes Corp., we recognize that every homeowner has distinct requirements and visions for their dream home, and we are here to help you achieve your objectives.

Design and Planning

Our bespoke design and planning services cater to your specific needs. Our team of experienced architects and designers will collaborate with you to create a blueprint that reflects your style, preferences, and budget. We take into account all aspects of the project, including layout, materials, and aesthetics, to ensure that every detail aligns with your vision.

New Construction

If you are considering building a new home, our team of experts can guide you through the entire process, from lot selection to home design and construction. We utilize only the highest quality materials and employ state-of-the-art construction techniques to guarantee a durable, energy-efficient, and visually appealing home.

Renovations and Additions

If you love your current home but require additional space or an updated appearance, our team can assist you. We can transform your existing space into a more functional and beautiful living area. Whether you need to add a new room, expand your kitchen or living room, or create a home office or gym, we can turn your dreams into reality.

Foundation Work

A solid foundation is crucial for any construction project, and our team possesses the knowledge and expertise to ensure that it is done correctly. We provide excavation, footings, foundation walls, and slab-on-grade foundation services, as well as repair and reinforcement for existing foundations.

Additional Services

We collaborate with you to design a space that caters to your specific needs and preferences.

  • Framing: Our highly skilled carpenters have vast experience in framing, encompassing the construction of walls, floors, and roofs. We use only premium materials to guarantee a sturdy and durable home.
  • Roofing: Our comprehensive roofing services include repair, replacement, and installation of various roofing systems such as asphalt, metal, tile, and flat roofing. We use top-quality materials and adhere to the highest workmanship standards to ensure a secure, efficient, and visually appealing roof.
  • Electrical and Plumbing: We offer an extensive range of electrical and plumbing services, including new installations, upgrades, and repairs. Our team of licensed electricians and plumbers will ensure that your systems are safe, efficient, and compliant with relevant codes.
  • Custom Carpentry: We can create custom cabinets, shelves, and other carpentry work to meet your specific storage needs.
  • Painting and Finishing Work: Our talented painters and finishers can provide the final touches to your project, including interior and exterior painting, trim work, flooring, and more. We use only premium paints and finishes to ensure your home's beauty endures for years to come.
